01 · Services What we do

i konstrukcyjne.

Precision TIG welding across aluminium, stainless and mild steel. Every weld explained before we strike the arc — clean prep, sound penetration, honest quotes.

01

TIG — Aluminium

Thin-gauge to structural aluminium. AC TIG with proper prep — no porosity, no contamination, clean toes.

02

TIG — Stainless Steel

Food-grade, exhaust, decorative. Back-purged where it matters. Coloured heat tint on request — clean weld every time.

03

TIG — Mild Steel

Brackets, frames, repairs. Strong fusion, neat beads. Ready for paint or powder coat without grinding marathons.

04

Custom Fabrication

One-offs and small batches. Bring a sketch or a CAD file — we'll cut, form and weld to your spec.

05

Repairs & Restoration

Cracked castings, broken brackets, classic vehicle bodywork. Sympathetic repairs that disappear under finish.

06

Mobile Callout

On-site welding across Gloucestershire. Generator and full kit travel with us — minimum job size applies.

07

Motorsport & Performance

Roll cages, manifolds, charge pipes, sumps. Race-spec finish where the regs and the eye both matter.

08

Bespoke Metalwork

Gates, railings, furniture, sculpture. Architectural stainless and steel pieces built to last decades.

03 · How it works Simple, honest process

No surprises.
Just straight talking.

Here's exactly what to expect when you bring a job to the workshop. No jargon, no pressure, no hidden costs.

Get in touch

Call, WhatsApp or use the form. Send a photo of the job — you'll get a rough idea right away.

Drop off or book mobile

Drop the job off at the workshop or book a mobile callout. Free parking on-site and easy access from the city.

Inspect & quote

Material confirmed, prep planned, firm quote given before the arc strikes. You decide.

Welded & ready

Clean weld, finished as agreed. You get a call as soon as it's ready and a walk-through of the job.
